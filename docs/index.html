PartyPulse

A Midnight-compatible party interrupt & cooldown tracker for World of Warcraft. Every client broadcasts its own casts so your whole party sees every kick in real time.

Install

  1. Download the zip above.
  2. Extract it into World of Warcraft/_retail_/Interface/AddOns/. You should end up with a PartyPulse folder (not a nested one).
  3. Launch WoW and enable PartyPulse in the AddOns list. Type /pulse to open settings.
